The Olinda Ferny Creek Junior Football Club is home to the mighty Young Bloods, one of the largest and most successful clubs in the Outer East Junior Football League. With 150+ players across 8 boys and girls teams, the club provides a positive environment for children to develop their skills through Australian Rules football.
